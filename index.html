My Journey So Far

I started this portfolio because I wanted one place to keep my projects, notes, and experiments. Earlier, I made small things and then forgot where I kept them. A portfolio helped me organize my work and gave me motivation to finish what I started.

When I first began, even basic HTML tags confused me. I made many mistakes with headings, links, and layouts. Then I started reading examples, testing code, and changing one thing at a time. Slowly I understood how structure, design, and behavior work together. This site became a learning journal where I can compare old and new versions and see real growth.

Why I Started Coding

I enjoy solving problems and creating things that other people can use. Coding gives me both. I like that one idea can become a real tool, game, or webpage using only logic and creativity. I am especially interested in computers, mathematics, and science, so programming feels like a natural mix of all my favorite subjects.
